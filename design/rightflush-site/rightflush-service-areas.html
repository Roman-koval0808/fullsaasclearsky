Headquarters
Timmins
Cochrane District, Northeastern Ontario

Our home base. Total Trades Solutions started here in 2018 and the owner still takes jobs in Timmins personally. We know the housing stock in this city — the 1950s bungalows in Schumacher, the 1970s builds in Mountjoy, the newer development on the south end. Emergency response within 15 minutes to most of Timmins proper.

Timmins plumbing
South Porcupine
~5 km east of Timmins

Within Timmins city limits. Response times are essentially the same as central Timmins. We see the same housing stock — older single-family homes with the same cold-weather plumbing challenges. All services available including emergency response.

South Porcupine plumbing
Iroquois Falls
~45 km east on Hwy 67

We serve Iroquois Falls regularly for both scheduled work and emergencies. The town has a significant proportion of older housing stock — particularly around the mill district — with galvanised supply lines that benefit from PEX upgrades. Emergency response approximately 45–60 minutes.

Iroquois Falls plumbing
Matheson
~60 km southeast on Hwy 101

Matheson and surrounding Black River-Matheson Township are part of our regular service rotation. Many properties here have well systems — we handle all plumbing connected to well infrastructure including pressure tanks, supply lines, and filtration. Emergency response approximately 60–75 minutes.

Matheson plumbing
Cochrane
~80 km north on Hwy 11

Cochrane is our northernmost regular service area. The town sits on the Abitibi River and experiences some of the most severe cold snap conditions in our coverage area — frozen pipe calls from Cochrane are disproportionately high in February. Emergency response approximately 60–90 minutes.

Cochrane plumbing
Kirkland Lake
~140 km south on Hwy 66

We serve Kirkland Lake for scheduled work and urgent non-emergency situations. For true emergencies in Kirkland Lake, we'll dispatch but the 90–120 minute response time means we'll also help you source local emergency plumbers as a parallel option if needed. Same flat-rate pricing applies.

Kirkland Lake plumbing
Kapuskasing
~140 km west on Hwy 11

Kapuskasing is at the far reach of our service area. We take scheduled jobs here regularly — particularly larger renovations and hot water system replacements that justify the drive. Emergency response is 90–120 minutes. Call us and we'll give you an honest assessment of whether we can help in time.

Kapuskasing plumbing
