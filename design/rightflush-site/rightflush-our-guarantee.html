Workmanship guarantee

If it's not right,
we come back.

Every Total Trades Solutions installation is backed by a written workmanship guarantee. If something we installed fails due to our work — we return to fix it. No charge. No questions.

This covers defects in workmanship — how we installed something, how we connected it, how we sealed it. It does not cover manufacturer part failure, damage caused after we left, or normal wear and tear. If you're ever unsure whether something is covered, call us — we'll tell you straight.

Workmanship guarantee terms
Installations — 1 year. All plumbing installations including water heaters, fixtures, pipe work, and connections. If our installation causes a failure within 12 months, we fix it at no charge.
Service calls — 90 days. Repairs, drain clearing, diagnostics, and service work. If the issue returns within 90 days due to our repair, we come back and fix it at no charge.
Parts — manufacturer warranty. Parts carry the original manufacturer warranty, which we register on your behalf where applicable. We'll assist with any manufacturer warranty claim.
No charge callbacks. If you call us about a guarantee issue, we diagnose it for free even if it turns out to be outside coverage. We won't send you a bill for showing up.
The fine print, plainly: The guarantee covers our work, not parts that fail independently or problems caused by events after we left (third-party damage, flooding, freezing of pipes we didn't work on). When in doubt — call us and we'll tell you whether it's covered before we visit.
Flat-rate pricing

The quote is
the invoice.

We price the job, not the hour. Before any work starts, you have a flat-rate price in writing. That number does not change unless the scope changes — and if it does, you approve it before we continue.

No hourly rate that stretches. No parts markups buried in the back of the invoice. No call-out fees layered on top. One number, agreed upfront, paid when the job is done to your satisfaction.

How flat-rate pricing works
We assess the full job first. Before quoting, we look at the problem, confirm what's needed, and price the complete scope — not an estimate of what we think it might be.
You approve before we start. The price is presented clearly. You decide whether to proceed. No pressure, no commitment until you say yes.
Scope changes are communicated immediately. If we open something up and find a problem that changes the scope, we stop, tell you, and get your approval before continuing.
Free estimates on jobs over $500. The estimate visit costs nothing. We assess, we quote, you decide. No call-out fee.
On FotoJobber quotes: photo quotes are accurate assessments based on what we can see. If we arrive and find the job is significantly different from what the photo showed, we tell you before starting. The quote is revised with your approval.
Licensing & insurance

Licensed. Insured.
No exceptions.

Every plumbing job performed by Total Trades Solutions is done by a fully licensed plumber under Ontario College of Trades certification. No unlicensed helpers on technical work. No subcontracting to unchecked trades.

Ontario College of Trades

Both Total Trades Solutions plumbers hold current Ontario College of Trades certification. Licence numbers available on request. We don't send anyone who isn't fully certified to do technical work.

TSSA gas certification

All gas fitting work in Ontario must be performed by a TSSA-certified contractor. Both Total Trades Solutions plumbers hold current TSSA certification. No gas work is done by anyone without it.

$2M liability insurance

Full commercial general liability insurance to $2 million. If something goes wrong on your property as a result of our work, you are covered. Insurance certificate available on request.

Permits pulled and managed

Any work requiring a permit — rough-in, gas lines, drain alterations — we pull the permit and manage the inspection process from start to sign-off. Permit cost is included in the quote.

Owner on the tools

The owner still takes jobs. You may well deal directly with the same person who started the company in 2018. Personal accountability on every job — not delegated down the chain.

Real person answers

During business hours, a real person answers every call. No voicemail, no phone tree. Emergency calls are answered 24/7. The person on the phone can answer your questions and book your job.
