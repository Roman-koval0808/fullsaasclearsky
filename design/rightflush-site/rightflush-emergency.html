What to do right now

While we're on our way — steps to protect your home in the next 15 minutes.

01
Shut off the water

Find your main water shutoff valve — usually in the basement near the foundation wall or under the kitchen sink. Turn clockwise to close. This stops flooding immediately.

02
Turn off the hot water tank

If you have a gas water heater, set the dial to Pilot. Electric? Switch off the breaker labelled Water Heater. Prevents the tank from running dry and burning out the element.

03
Move what you can

Get valuables, documents, and electronics off the floor. Water damage spreads fast — furniture, rugs, and stored items can be saved if moved quickly.

04
We're on our way

A licensed plumber is dispatched the moment you call. They'll confirm their ETA and call you when they're 5 minutes out. Keep your phone close.
