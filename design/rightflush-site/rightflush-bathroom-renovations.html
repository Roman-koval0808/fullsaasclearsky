What we handle

Full bathroom plumbing.
Start to finish.

Total Trades Solutions handles the complete plumbing scope on your bathroom renovation — working alongside your contractor or managing the whole project.

Rough-in Plumbing

All supply and drain lines repositioned or extended to suit your new layout. We work from the structural plan and coordinate with your framing timeline.

Fixture Installation

Toilet, vanity, sink, shower, tub, bath filler, and all associated trim — supplied by you or sourced through us at trade price.

Hot Water Systems

Upgrade or relocate your hot water source as part of the renovation. Tankless options, recirculation pumps, and zone valves all available.

Supply Line Upgrades

Replace old galvanised or copper supply lines with modern PEX. Quieter, freeze-resistant, and built to last 50+ years in Northern Ontario conditions.

Fan and Vent Work

Exhaust fan installation and duct routing. We coordinate with your electrician on the fan unit and handle the duct run to exterior.

Permit and Inspection

We pull the required permits for all plumbing work and manage the inspection process from start to sign-off. You never deal with the building department.
