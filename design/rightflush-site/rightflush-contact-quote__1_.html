FAQ

Common questions
before contacting us.

Do you charge for estimates?
Free estimates on all jobs over $500. For smaller jobs — a dripping tap or simple repair — we can usually quote over the phone or via FotoJobber without a visit. There's no call-out fee for estimates on larger work.
How does flat-rate pricing work?
Before any work starts, we quote the full job as a single flat price. That's the number on the invoice — no hourly rate, no hidden add-ons. If we encounter something unexpected, we tell you and get approval before continuing.
How fast will I get a response?
FotoJobber quotes are reviewed and sent back within 2 hours during business hours. Appointment requests are confirmed by phone same day, or next morning if submitted after 5pm. For emergencies, call directly — we answer 24/7 and dispatch within 15 minutes.
What areas do you serve?
Timmins and surrounding area — Cochrane, South Porcupine, Iroquois Falls, Kapuskasing, Matheson, and Kirkland Lake. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, call and ask — we may still be able to help depending on the job scope.
Do you pull permits?
Yes. Any plumbing work requiring a permit — new rough-in, drain alterations, gas line work — we pull it and manage the inspection. You never deal with the building department. The permit cost is included in the quote.
What's the workmanship guarantee?
If anything we installed or repaired develops a problem caused by our work, we come back and fix it at no charge. This covers the workmanship — not part failure from manufacturer defects or damage caused by the homeowner after handover.
